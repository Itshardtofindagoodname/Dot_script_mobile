Welcome to DotScript
Where all your medical questions are answered.
DotScript is a web-based medical information system designed to provide users with reliable and easily accessible information about various diseases, drugs, and symptoms. It serves as a one-stop resource for individuals seeking accurate medical information, aiding in self-education and informed decision-making about their health. The system features a chatbot capable of answering common medical queries, enhancing user engagement and addressing day-to-day health concerns.
The chatbot uses natural language processing (NLP) algorithms to understand and interpret user queries. It then retrieves relevant information from the database and formulates clear, concise responses. The chatbot can provide guidance on a wide range of health-related topics, helping users with everyday medical queries and concerns.
